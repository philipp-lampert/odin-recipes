Kaiserschmarrn

Description

As a main course or desert - This Austrian classic is loved by everyone. We'll show you how to make it yourself!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 125g flour
  • 125ml milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 40g sugar
  • 80g raisins
  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • 1 tbsp. icing sugar

Steps

  1. Separate eggs
  2. Make a dough with the egg yolks, flour, milk, salt, baking powder and sugar
  3. Beat the egg whites and add them to the dough
  4. Add raisins
  5. Heat butter in a pan and make a pancake
  6. Turn the pancake and fry it on the other side while adding butter
  7. Break the pancake into pieces and add icing sugar
