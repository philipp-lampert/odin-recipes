Maultaschen

Description

A German dish with a long history and a must-try for everyone interested in German culture!

Ingredients

  • 400g flour
  • 125ml water
  • 1 tbsp. vinegar
  • 5 tbsp. oil
  • 400g spinach
  • 1 onion
  • 1.5 buns
  • 200g ground meat
  • 200g sausage
  • 1 tbsp. parsley
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5l meat broth
  • 1 tbsp. chives

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, 1 tsp. salt, water, vinegar and oil and knead it into a dough
  2. Let it rest for 20 min and proceed with the next steps for the filling

  3. Cook the spinach for 3 minutes
  4. Cut the onion into small pieces
  5. Soak the buns in cold water
  6. Chop the spinach into small pieces
  7. Dry the buns and mix with ground meat, sausage, onion, parsley, spinach, eggs, salt and pepper

  8. Spread the dough from the first step and cut into 15cm big squares
  9. Add one tbsp. of the filling to each square of dough
  10. Fold the dough (rectangle or triangle) and press well on the borders
  11. Make the meat broth boil and add the Maultaschen for 10min
  12. They are ready when they swim on the surface

  13. Serve with meat broth and chives
