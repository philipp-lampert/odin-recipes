Paella

Description

This traditional Spanish rice dish is an incredibly tasty addition to your cooking repertoire!

Ingredients

  • 1 pckg. seafood
  • 250g rice
  • 80g peas
  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 red pepper
  • 6 tbsp. olive oil
  • Saffron
  • Paprika powder
  • 700ml vegetable broth
  • 100ml white wine

Steps

  1. Heat the olive ol and saut√© the onion together with half of the garlic
  2. Add the rice and season with saffron and pepper
  3. Add wine and broth
  4. Cook while stirring
  5. Heat olive oil in a second pan
  6. Add the seafood, peas, pepper (cut in stripes) and garlic
  7. Fry for a short time and add to the rice
  8. Let it cook until most of the broth has dissipated
  9. Season with paprika powder, pepper and salt
